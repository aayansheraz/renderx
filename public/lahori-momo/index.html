FAQ

Sawaal? Jawaab.

How do I place an order?

Add your favourites to the basket and tap “Checkout on WhatsApp” — your order arrives to us as a ready message on 0317-7677686. Confirm your address and we start folding.

Do you deliver? What about delivery charges?

Yes — we deliver across Lahore via rider. Delivery is free on orders above Rs 5,000; below that, standard rider charges apply depending on your area.

Are your momos halal?

100%. All our chicken is sourced fresh daily from certified halal suppliers, and everything is prepared in-house at our stall.

Can you cater parties and events?

Bilkul! Our Party Box feeds a crowd, and for bigger events we do custom platters. Message us on WhatsApp and we'll plan it together.

Where exactly is the stall?

Johar Town, Block 80 K, Lahore. Tap “Get Directions” in the Visit Us section — Google Maps will bring you straight to the steam.
